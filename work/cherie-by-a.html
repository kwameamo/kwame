The Brief

Cherié by A is a luxury feminine brand with a specific vision: refined without being cold, elevated without being inaccessible. The founder needed an identity that would resonate with women who have taste, not just money — women who would recognise craft and intention.

The brief was to translate a very personal, very precise aesthetic into a complete brand system — from the mark to the packaging to the way the brand speaks. Everything had to feel like it belonged to the same hand.

The Work

The identity centres on a wordmark with fine-tuned letterforms — every curve deliberate, every space considered. The mark works at any scale, from embossed on a box to printed on tissue paper.

Packaging was designed as an experience, not a container. The unboxing sequence — tissue, ribbon, card, product — was choreographed to match the emotional expectation of a luxury purchase. Every material choice, every weight of paper, every finish was specified to serve that moment.

Art direction for campaigns and product photography defined a consistent visual signature: natural light, specific skin tones, objects with presence. Not aspirational in the distant sense — aspirational in the tangible sense.

The Outcome

Cherié by A has a brand that operates cohesively across product, digital, and campaign. The identity has held through multiple product launches without needing reinvention — which is the measure of a well-built system.

The ongoing creative direction relationship means the brand keeps evolving without losing what makes it recognisable.
