The Brief

Organisations without a proper web presence are invisible. For Akrofi Bene Est — operating both as a commercial entity and through its foundation arm — a functional, credible website wasn't optional. It was necessary for partners, stakeholders, and the public to find and trust what the organisation does.

The brief was to build a web presence that represented both sides of the organisation clearly and cohesively, used the brand identity developed for the foundation, and worked reliably for all users regardless of device or connection quality.

The Work

The site was built to be fast, clean, and easy to navigate. The information architecture separates the commercial entity from the foundation clearly, without making the organisation feel fragmented. Visitors reach what they're looking for without confusion.

Design extends from the brand identity established in the Foundation project — consistent typography, colour system, and visual language across both the site and the offline materials. The site doesn't feel like a different entity from the stationery.

Development prioritised performance and reliability. The codebase is lightweight, the site loads quickly on mobile networks, and the build is maintainable. No unnecessary dependencies. No over-engineering.

The Outcome

Akrofi Bene Est has a digital presence that works. The site is live, fast, responsive, and consistent with the wider brand identity. Partners and stakeholders can find what they need; the organisation presents with the credibility it deserves.

This project completes a full-service delivery for Akrofi Bene Est — from brand identity to digital presence, both coming from the same designer with the same understanding of the organisation.
